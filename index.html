Introduction

Welcome to the premier community for doubles tennis enthusiasts in Gainesville and Haymarket. This year, we are launching a flexible doubles league designed to foster a vibrant local tennis culture.

Our mission is simple: Have fun, build community, and sharpen your match-play tactics. Whether you're looking for competitive intensity or social rallies, there’s a spot for you on the court.

How It Works

  • Free Entry: Any interested players may join the league at no cost.
  • Ultimate Flexibility: Challenge other teams whenever your schedule allows. No fixed weekly times.
  • Multiple Partnerships: Players are encouraged to join multiple teams.
  • Stay Connected: We use the TeamReach App for communication and match finding.
  • Report Scores: Submit your match results to be added to the seasonal leaderboard.

Match Rules & Etiquette

To ensure consistency across the league, please follow these standard play guidelines for all matches:

Scoring Format

  • Main Play: Best 2 out of 3 sets.
  • Set Tiebreak: 7-point tiebreak if a set reaches 6-6.
  • Deciding Set: If sets are split 1-1, play a 10-point "Super Tiebreak" to determine the winner.

On-Court Protocol

  • No "First In": All warm-up serves must be completed before the match begins. No practice serves during game play.
  • Ball Policy: Matches should be played with a fresh can of balls. Used balls are only acceptable if all four players explicitly agree before starting.
  • Line Calls: Respect your opponent's calls. There is a zero-tolerance policy for arguing line calls. If you didn't see it out clearly, it's in.
